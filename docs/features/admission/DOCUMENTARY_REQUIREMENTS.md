## 1. Critical Rules That Apply to All Grade Levels

Before examining grade-level specifics, these rules from DO 017, s. 2025 apply universally across all grades and all learner types.

### Rule 1 — PSA Birth Certificate Is Submitted Once

All learners shall only submit a Birth Certificate ONCE throughout their stay in basic education (K to 12). This is mandated by RA 11909 (Permanent Validity of the Certificates of Live Birth, Death, and Marriage Act).

**What this means in practice:**
- If a learner's PSA BC is already on file at the school from a prior year, the registrar does not ask for it again
- If the learner is transferring from another school, the PSA BC was submitted at their previous school — the receiving school can request it via DepEd LIS or accept a copy for verification
- The registrar notes the PSA BC reference number in the learner's record — physical original stays in the file permanently

### Rule 2 — Documents Are for Verification, Not Collection During Early Registration

Although documents shall be required for presentation to early register learners, these documents are not subject to collection and are only for verification purposes.

During **Phase 1 (Early Registration)**, the registrar checks documents — they do not retain them. Physical documents are collected and filed during **Phase 2 (Actual Enrollment)**.

### Rule 3 — No Learner May Be Refused Enrollment for Missing Documents

A learner who cannot present complete documents on enrollment day is enrolled on a **temporary basis** and given until August 31 of the current school year to submit outstanding documents. Missing documents are never grounds for outright rejection.

### Rule 4 — SF10 (Form 137 / Permanent Record) Is NOT an Enrollment Requirement

SF10 is not an initial requirement when enrolling a student as a transferee or incoming Grade 7 and 11. Instead, submit the SF10 later once the student is temporarily enrolled or transitioned to the secondary level.

SF10 is the learner's cumulative academic record — the school-to-school transfer of this document is handled between institutions through the LIS portal after enrollment, not before.

### Rule 5 — Enrollment in Public Schools Is Free

No enrollment fee, processing fee, or any other payment may be collected from any learner at any grade level as a condition of enrollment in a public school.

## 2. The Two Enrollment Phases — What They Require

|---|---|---|

| **Who must undergo this** | Grade 7 · Grade 11 · All Transferees · Balik-Aral | Everyone (Grades 7–12) |

| **Outcome** | Pre-registered (reserved slot) | Officially enrolled of record |

## 3. Secondary Documents — When PSA BC is Not Available

If the PSA Birth Certificate is not yet available at the time of enrollment, any of the following may be accepted as substitute proof of identity. In the absence of a PSA Birth Certificate, the parent or guardian must submit a Birth Certificate (late registration) from the local civil registrar or a barangay certification containing the basic information of the child such as name, name of parents, date of birth, and sex.

The complete list of acceptable secondary documents under DO 017, s. 2025:

| # | Secondary Document | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Birth Certificate (late registration) from the Local Civil Registrar | Must contain name, parents' names, date of birth, sex |
| 2 | Barangay Certification | Must contain name, parents' names, date of birth, sex |
| 3 | Baptismal Certificate | As proof of date of birth and name |
| 4 | Certificate of Foundling Registration (RA 11222) | For foundlings without birth registration |
| 5 | Valid passport | For nationality and identity |
| 6 | Any government-issued ID showing name and birthdate | — |
| 7 | Affidavit of Two Disinterested Persons | Attesting to the learner's identity and date of birth |

**Deadline for PSA BC submission:** If a secondary document is used at enrollment, the learner is temporarily enrolled. The PSA Birth Certificate must be submitted by **October 31** of the current school year.

## 4. School Form Reference Guide

Understanding the school forms referenced throughout this document:

| Form | Official Name | Old Name | Issued By | Contains |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **SF9** | Learner's Progress Report Card | Form 138 (Report Card) | Class Adviser | Quarterly grades, attendance, character ratings, promotion status — covers one school year |
| **SF10-JHS** | Learner's Permanent Academic Record — JHS | Form 137 (Cumulative Record) | Class Adviser / Registrar | All grades from Grades 7 to 10, eligibility records, transfer certification |
| **SF10-SHS** | Learner's Permanent Academic Record — SHS | Form 137 (Cumulative Record) | Class Adviser / Registrar | All grades from Grades 11 to 12 |
| **BEEF** | Basic Education Enrollment Form | (None — new under DO 017) | Applicant / Registrar | All enrollment data — the official enrollment form |
| **Confirmation Slip** | Enrollment Confirmation Slip | — | School | Used by Grades 8–10 and Grade 12 continuing learners to confirm enrollment |

The SF9 is issued at the end of each term or semester, containing the grades received by the student. Transferees from public and private schools who failed to submit their SF9 will only be regarded by the receiving school as temporarily enrolled learners until they have submitted the required documents for enrollment.

## 5. Grade 7 — Incoming Junior High School

Grade 7 is a **Phase 1 Early Registration priority**. Incoming Grade 7 learners must go through early registration from the last Saturday of January to the last Friday of February.

### 5.1 Who Qualifies

For Grade 7: Grade 6 Graduate or Grade 6 Candidate for Graduation — required document: SF9 (Formerly Form 138) Grade 6. Also qualified: PEPT or A&E Elementary Certification Passer — required: PEPT or A&E Certificate of Rating/Presentation of Portfolio Assessment (PPA) Certificate, plus PSA or NSO Birth Certificate or any of the Secondary Documents.

### 5.2 Phase 1 — Early Registration Documents

Documents are **presented for verification only** — not yet collected by the registrar.

| Document | Required? | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SF9 (Grade 6 Report Card)** | ✅ Primary | Proof of Grade 6 completion | Must be signed by the Grade 6 school head. If unavailable: school certification letter from the elementary principal |
| PSA Birth Certificate | ✅ (once-only) | Identity verification | Only if this is the first time the learner submits to this school |
| Secondary document | If PSA BC unavailable | Substitute identity proof | See Section 3 |

**For PEPT/A&E passers:** PEPT or A&E Certificate of Rating or PPA Certificate **plus** PSA BC or secondary document.

### 5.3 Phase 2 — Actual Enrollment Documents

Documents are collected and filed permanently.

| Document | Required? | Deadline | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **BEEF (Basic Education Enrollment Form)** | ✅ | At enrollment | The official DepEd enrollment form — filled out completely |
| **SF9 (Grade 6 Report Card)** — original or certified true copy | ✅ | At enrollment or by Aug 31 | Original preferred; certified copy accepted if original is with sending school |
| **PSA Birth Certificate** — original | ✅ (once-only) | At enrollment or by Oct 31 | Collected once; filed permanently in SF10 |
| Privacy consent (RA 10173) | ✅ | At enrollment | Signed by parent/guardian — physical signature on consent form |

### 5.4 What Is NOT Required for Grade 7

| ❌ NOT Required | Reason |
|---|---|
| SF10 (Form 137) from elementary | SF10 is transmitted school-to-school via LIS portal after enrollment |
| Good Moral Character certificate | Not required by DO 017, s. 2025 in public schools |
| Report of Rating (RR) | Not applicable at this level |
| Barangay clearance | Not an enrollment requirement |
| Medical certificate | Not required unless school has health-specific program requirements |

## 6. Grade 8 — Continuing JHS Learner

Grade 8 learners are **pre-registered** — they are continuing learners with existing LIS records from their Grade 7 year at the same school. Incoming learners for Grades 2-6, Grades 8-10, and Grade 12 are considered pre-registered.

### 6.1 Phase 1 — Early Registration

**Not required.** Grade 8 continuing learners are automatically pre-registered in the LIS. No early registration visit is needed.

### 6.2 Phase 2 — Actual Enrollment Documents

| Document | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Confirmation Slip (Annex C)** | ✅ | Simple form confirming the learner's intent to continue at this school for the next school year |
| SF9 (Grade 7 Report Card) | ❌ (not required) | Already on file from the previous year — the school retains this |
| PSA Birth Certificate | ❌ (not required) | Already on file from Grade 7 enrollment — once-only submission |

**The Confirmation Slip is the only document a Grade 8 continuing learner needs to bring.** The school already has all other records.

### 6.3 Exception — Grade 8 Transferee

A learner who enrolled at a different school for Grade 7 and is now transferring to this school for Grade 8 follows the **Transferee** requirements in Section 11 — not the continuing learner requirements above.

## 9. Grade 11 — Incoming Senior High School

Grade 11 is a **Phase 1 Early Registration priority** — the same as Grade 7. All incoming Grade 11 learners (both new SHS entrants and JHS completers transitioning to SHS) must undergo Early Registration.

### 9.1 Who Qualifies

- Grade 10 Completer (or Grade 10 Candidate Completer from any DepEd school)
- PEPT or A&E Junior High School Certification Passer

### 9.2 Phase 1 — Early Registration Documents

Documents **presented for verification only** — not yet collected.

| Document | Required? | Purpose | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SF9 (Grade 10 Report Card)** | ✅ Primary | Proof of Grade 10 completion | Must be signed by the JHS school head. If unavailable: school certification letter from the JHS principal |
| PSA Birth Certificate | ✅ (once-only) | Identity verification | Only if transferring from a different school where it was not previously submitted |
| Secondary document | If PSA BC unavailable | Substitute | See Section 3 |

**Additional for STEM Applicants (during SCP Early Registration):**

| Document | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **SF9 (Grade 10 Report Card)** showing Science and Mathematics grades | ✅ | STEM applicants need Grade 10 Science ≥ 85 and Math ≥ 85 — registrar verifies grades on the SF9 |

**For PEPT/A&E passers:** PEPT or A&E Certificate of Rating **plus** PSA BC or secondary document.

### 9.3 Phase 2 — Actual Enrollment Documents

| Document | Required? | Deadline | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **BEEF (Basic Education Enrollment Form)** | ✅ | At enrollment | Filled out for Grade 11 — includes SHS Track and Elective Cluster declaration |
| **SF9 (Grade 10 Report Card)** — original or certified true copy | ✅ | At enrollment or by Aug 31 | Must show completion/passing of Grade 10 |
| **PSA Birth Certificate** — original | ✅ (once-only) | At enrollment or by Oct 31 | If not previously on file at this school |
| Privacy consent (RA 10173) | ✅ | At enrollment | Signed by parent/guardian |

### 9.4 SHS Track and Cluster Declaration

The BEEF for Grade 11 includes additional fields not present in the Grade 7 form:
- SHS Track selection: Academic or Technical-Professional (TechPro)
- Elective Cluster selection (loaded from the school's configured strands)
- For STEM cluster: Grade 10 Science and Mathematics final grades (written on the form, verified against SF9)

### 9.5 What Is NOT Required for Grade 11

| ❌ NOT Required | Reason |
|---|---|
| SF10-JHS (Form 137 JHS record) | Not required at enrollment — transmitted via LIS after enrollment |
| Grade 10 Diploma / Certificate of Completion | SF9 is the primary eligibility document — diploma is separate |
| Good Moral Character certificate | Not required by DO 017, s. 2025 in public schools |

## 11. Transferees — All Grade Levels

A transferee is a learner enrolling from a **different school** — whether public or private, within the Philippines. The transferee process applies regardless of the grade level they are entering.

Transferees from public and private schools in the Philippines who failed to submit the SF9 (formerly Form 138) during early registration or upon enrollment shall only be temporarily enrolled until the submission of required documents on or before August 31st of the current school year.

### 11.1 Phase 1 — Early Registration (Transferees)

All transferees to public schools are included in the Early Registration requirement. Transferees must undergo early registration even if transferring to Grades 8, 9, 10, or 12 — where continuing learners are otherwise pre-registered.

| Document | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **SF9 (Report Card from sending school)** — most recent | ✅ Primary | Must be signed by the school head of the sending school. Shows the last grade level completed and final grades |
| PSA Birth Certificate | ✅ (once-only) | If not previously submitted to this school |
| Secondary document | If PSA BC unavailable | See Section 3 |

### 11.2 Phase 2 — Actual Enrollment Documents

| Document | Required? | Deadline | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **BEEF** | ✅ | At enrollment | Mark Learner Type as "Transferee" |
| **SF9 (most recent Report Card)** | ✅ | At enrollment or by Aug 31 | Most recent report card from sending school |
| **PSA Birth Certificate** | ✅ (once-only) | At enrollment or by Oct 31 | If not previously on file |
| Privacy consent (RA 10173) | ✅ | At enrollment | — |
| **Affidavit of Undertaking** (if applicable) | Conditional | At enrollment | Required only when transferee has unpaid tuition/fees at the previous private school — a physical document between the parent and the previous school; not collected by the receiving public school |

### 11.3 What Registrars Must NOT Require from Transferees

| ❌ NOT Required | Policy Basis |
|---|---|
| SF10 (Form 137 / Permanent Record) | Not an enrollment requirement — transmitted via LIS after enrollment |
| Clearance from previous school | Not required by DO 017, s. 2025 |
| Good Moral Certificate | Not required |
| Proof that fees at previous school are paid | Schools cannot deny a transferee's enrollment due to unpaid private school fees. |
| Transfer certificate | Not required at enrollment — part of the SF10 |

### 11.4 SF10 Transfer Process (Post-Enrollment)

After the transferee is enrolled, the receiving school requests the SF10 (Permanent Record) from the sending school:

1. Receiving school initiates a transfer request through the **DepEd LIS Portal — Tracking/Transfer Facility**
2. Sending school processes and transmits the scanned SF10 via the school's official DepEd email (`schoolid@deped.gov.ph`)
3. Target turnaround: 30 days from the request date
4. Until SF10 is received, the learner's prior academic records are tracked through the LIS system using the LRN

## 12. Balik-Aral (Returning Learners) — All Grade Levels

A Balik-Aral learner has been out of school for **at least one year**. They may have incomplete records, missing documents, or no LRN in the system.

### 12.1 Phase 1 — Early Registration (Balik-Aral)

Balik-Aral learners are included in Phase 1 Early Registration requirements.

For Balik-Aral Learners at any grade level — qualified through PEPT Passer or A&E Certification Passer — required documents: PSA or NSO Birth Certificate or any of the Secondary Documents, plus SF9 (Report Card), School Form 10 (Formerly Form 137), or PEPT/A&E Certificate of Rating/PPA Certificate.

### 12.2 Document Flexibility for Balik-Aral Learners

Balik-Aral learners often have incomplete documentation. The policy is deliberately flexible:

| Situation | Acceptable Document | Registrar Action |
|---|---|---|
| Has last SF9 | SF9 from last year attended | Verify grades; proceed normally |
| No SF9 but has an old report card | Any report card from any prior year | Accept as proof of last grade attended |
| No report card at all | School certification letter from previously attended school OR barangay certification of last grade attended | Enroll provisionally; coordinate with SDO |
| No LRN | Learner registers as new if truly no prior LIS record | SDO assigns LRN; registrar notes exception |
| No PSA BC | Any secondary document from Section 3 | Temporarily enrolled; submit by Oct 31 |

### 12.3 Phase 2 — Actual Enrollment Documents

| Document | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **BEEF** | ✅ | Mark Learner Type as "Returning (Balik-Aral)" |
| **Most recent academic record** | ✅ (flexible) | SF9, school certification letter, barangay certification — whatever is available |
| **PSA Birth Certificate** | ✅ (once-only, flexible) | Secondary document accepted; submit PSA BC when available |
| Privacy consent (RA 10173) | ✅ | — |

**Core principle:** Never refuse a Balik-Aral learner due to documentation gaps. Enroll provisionally and coordinate with the SDO to resolve records through the LIS.

## 13. Learners with Disability (LWD) — Special Notes

For Special Needs Education Program learners — at least five years old at the beginning of the SY — required documents are: PSA or NSO Birth Certificate or any of the Secondary Documents, and PWD I.D. or Medical evaluation and diagnosis from a Licensed Medical Specialist, if available.

**Key rule:** A learner's disability status is collected for the purpose of providing appropriate educational support and SPED referral — **not** for restricting or conditioning enrollment.

| Document | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| PWD ID | ✅ (if available) | Issued by the local SWDO — optional, not blocking |
| Medical evaluation from Licensed Medical Specialist | ✅ (if available) | For SPED referral and accommodation planning |
| Standard enrollment documents for grade level | ✅ | Same as non-LWD requirements for their grade level |

**If no PWD ID or medical document is available:** Enroll the learner normally. The LWD classification can be updated later when documentation is obtained. The registrar coordinates with the school's SPED coordinator and the SDO for support services.

## 14. What Registrars Must NEVER Require

DO 017, s. 2025 explicitly prohibits using the following as conditions for enrollment in public schools:

| ❌ Prohibited Requirement | Policy Basis | Correct Action Instead |
|---|---|---|
| PSA Birth Certificate at every enrollment | RA 11909 — once-only submission | Check records; request only if not on file |
| SF10 (Form 137) as enrollment requirement | DO 017, s. 2025 §VII — not an initial requirement | Request via LIS post-enrollment |
| Good Moral Character certificate | DO 017, s. 2025 §III | No certificate required in public schools |
| Proof of payment of fees at previous school | DO 017, s. 2025 §5.5 | Enroll; assist with Affidavit of Undertaking |
| Clearance from previous school | Not in DO 017, s. 2025 | Not required |
| Barangay clearance | Not an enrollment requirement | Not required |
| Medical certificate (standard enrollment) | Not required unless SPED/SCP-specific | Not required |
| Any enrollment fee | RA 10533 — free basic education | Report to SDO if any school requires payment |
| Any requirement beyond the minimum documents | DO 017, s. 2025 §II | Follow minimum requirements only |
| Rejection of LWD learner | RA 7277 — Magna Carta for PWDs | Enroll and coordinate with SPED coordinator |

## 15. Temporary Enrollment — When Documents Are Missing

Only learners with a first date of attendance and complete documentary requirements shall be considered officially enrolled. When documents are incomplete, the learner is temporarily enrolled.

### What Temporary Enrollment Means

A temporarily enrolled learner:
- Attends classes normally from day one
- Is recorded in the class list and attendance
- Is NOT yet officially enrolled of record
- **Cannot be promoted** to the next grade level if documents remain incomplete at year-end
- **Cannot receive official school documents** (diploma, Certificate of Completion, SF9, SF10) until officially enrolled

### Deadlines for Submitting Missing Documents

| Document | Deadline |
|---|---|
| PSA Birth Certificate | **October 31** of the current school year |
| SF9 (Report Card from sending school, for transferees) | **August 31** of the current school year |
| All other outstanding enrollment documents | August 31 of the current school year |

### Registrar's Role in Temporary Enrollment

1. Enroll the learner — do not refuse entry
2. Mark the learner as "Temporarily Enrolled" in the LIS
3. Record which documents are outstanding and the expected submission date
4. Follow up with the parent/guardian before the August 31 deadline
5. Update to "Officially Enrolled" in the LIS once all documents are received

## 17. System Implementation Notes

These notes describe how the document requirements map to the system's data model and workflow.

### 17.1 BEEF Fields Map to the Applicant Model

The BEEF is the data source for the `Applicant` Prisma model. When a parent submits the online form (`/apply`) or the registrar enters a walk-in application (`/f2f-admission`), they are filling out the digital equivalent of the BEEF.

### 17.2 Document Verification is Physical — Not Stored as Files

The system does **not** store scanned copies of documents. Document verification happens physically at the registrar's window. The system stores:
- `psaBcNumber` — the PSA BC reference number noted by the registrar
- `lastSchoolAttended`, `lastGradeCompleted`, `generalAverage` — key data from the SF9
- `privacyConsentGiven: true` — the registrar's attestation that the physical consent form was signed

### 17.3 Learner Type Field Maps to Document Requirements

The `Applicant.learnerType` field (`NEW_ENROLLEE`, `TRANSFEREE`, `RETURNING`, `CONTINUING`) determines which documents the registrar expects to see:

```
NEW_ENROLLEE   → SF9 of last grade completed + PSA BC
TRANSFEREE     → SF9 from sending school + PSA BC (+ Affidavit of Undertaking if applicable)
RETURNING      → Any academic record available + PSA BC (flexible)
CONTINUING     → Confirmation Slip only (handled separately from the BEEF-based admission flow)
```

### 17.4 Phase 1 vs Phase 2 in the System

The system's `ApplicationStatus` enum reflects both phases:

| System Status | Phase | What It Represents |
|---|---|---|
| `PENDING` | Phase 1 | Application submitted; registrar reviewing documents |
| `APPROVED` | Phase 1 complete | Documents verified; section assigned; pre-registered |
| `ENROLLED` | Phase 2 complete | Physical documents filed; officially enrolled of record |

The `APPROVED → ENROLLED` transition corresponds to the registrar completing Phase 2 document verification in June.

### 17.5 Temporary Enrollment in the System

Currently, the system uses `PENDING` status for applications awaiting document completion. Learners who are enrolled provisionally (documents pending) are tracked via the physical enrollment logbook and the school's LIS entry — not a separate system status. The registrar notes which documents are outstanding when saving the application.
