ENROLLPRO BATCH SECTIONING ALGORITHM (SCP & BEC)
-----------------------------------------------

The EnrollPro system utilizes a tiered, priority-based autonomous sorting engine for batch sectioning, triggered once a learner's status reaches 'ENROLLED'. The hierarchy ensures that specialized programs are filled first, followed by academic ranking for top-tier sections, and finally even distribution for the general population.

HIEARCHY OF PLACEMENT:

1. PRIORITY 1: SCP PLACEMENT (HARD CAPS)
   - Target Pool: Learners tagged as [SCP]_QUALIFIED (e.g., STE_QUALIFIED, SPA_QUALIFIED, SPS_QUALIFIED).
   - Selection Logic: Qualified learners are assigned strictly to their respective Special Curricular Program sections.
   - Constraints: Class sizes are locked to program-specific maximums (Hard Caps). No overflow is allowed into SCP sections.

2. PRIORITY 2: BEC HOMOGENEOUS PLACEMENT (STAR SECTIONS)
   - Target Pool: Remaining learners in the ENROLLED pool (Basic Education Curriculum).
   - Target Sections: Sections 1 through 5 (designated as "Star Sections").
   - Selection Logic: Learners are ranked by their 'final_general_average' in descending order.
   - Assignment: Top-ranked learners are placed into Sections 1-5 to maintain homogeneous academic excellence in these groups.

3. PRIORITY 3: BEC HETEROGENEOUS PLACEMENT (STANDARD SECTIONS)
   - Target Pool: All remaining BEC learners not placed in Star Sections.
   - Target Sections: Section 6 and beyond.
   - Selection Logic: Round-Robin (Even) distribution.
   - Goal: Ensures balanced class sizes and a heterogeneous mix of learners across all standard sections.

-----------------------------------------------
PHASE 1: SCP RANKING ALGORITHM (SCREENING)
-----------------------------------------------
Before sectioning occurs, SCP applicants are screened using the following weighted formula:

Final Score = (Assessment Score * 60%) + (Interview Score * 20%) + (Q1/Q2 Average * 20%)

- Assessment: Specialized tests (Written, Audition, Physical, Aptitude) depending on the SCP.
- Interview: Qualitative evaluation by SCP Coordinators or Coaches.
- Q1/Q2 Avg: Initial academic performance from Grade 6.

Learners who meet the cutoff are tagged as [SCP]_QUALIFIED for Priority 1 sectioning. Remaining applicants are shifted to the BEC pathway.
