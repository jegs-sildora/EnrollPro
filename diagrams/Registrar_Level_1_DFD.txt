================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING (DEPED CONCISE)
================================================================================

PROCESS: P6.0 Verify & Screen Applications
-> Inbound from Actor: [IN] Requirement Veridical & Assessment Scores (Checklist of PSA/SF9/Moral and screening exam results)
<- Outbound to Actor: [OUT] Verified Roster & Compliance Alerts (Official list of qualified applicants and missing document notifications)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D11: application_checklists ([IN to DB] BEEF Compliance Booleans)
   - D2: early_registration_applications ([IN to DB] Application Eligibility State)
   - D12: early_registration_assessments ([IN to DB] Qualitative Screening Results)
   - D10: audit_logs ([IN to DB] DPA-Compliant Verification Audit)

PROCESS: P7.0 Manage Sectioning & Enrollment
-> Inbound from Actor: [IN] BOSY Sectioning Plan (Grade-level section assignments and sectioning method)
<- Outbound to Actor: [OUT] Official Class Rosters & SF1 Data (Preliminary School Form 1 lists and current room capacities)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D13: enrollment_records ([IN to DB] Definitive Section Linkage)
   - D5: enrollment_applications ([IN to DB] FINAL ENROLLED Status)
   - D14: sections ([IN to DB] Real-time Capacity Updates)
   - D10: audit_logs ([IN to DB] Placement Audit Log)

PROCESS: P8.0 Finalize EOSY Promotional Status
-> Inbound from Actor: [IN] EOSY Finalization Data (General average, promotional status, SF1 remarks, and physical BMI data)
<- Outbound to Actor: [OUT] SF1 Register & SF5 Promotion Master (The finalized School Form 1 and the definitive Report on Promotion)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D13: enrollment_records ([IN to DB] Final Promotional Data)
   - D14: sections ([IN to DB] Section Finality Lock)
   - D15: health_records ([IN to DB] SF8 Basic Health Metrics)
   - D10: audit_logs ([IN to DB] Year-End Finalization Log)
