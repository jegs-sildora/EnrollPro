================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING (SENIOR ARCHITECT AUDIT)
================================================================================

PROCESS: P6.0 Verify & Screen Applications
-> Inbound from Registrar: [Document Compliance Submission & Assessment Scores] (Noun: Packet of compliance flags (PSA, SF9, Good Moral Certificate) and numerical SCP assessment scores)
<- Inbound from DB: [Pending Application Data] (Reads D2 early_registration_applications to identify the applicant pool awaiting verification)
<- Inbound from DB: [Rubric Criteria] (Reads D14 scp_program_configs to validate submitted scores against active program admission logic)
<- Inbound from DB: [Prior Compliance Data] (Reads D15 application_checklists and D16 early_registration_assessments to compile the summary report)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [Compliance Verification Report] (Noun: Roster of qualified applicants and list of missing document notifications)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D15: application_checklists (Persists boolean compliance flags for each required document)
   - D2: early_registration_applications (Updates eligibility status to QUALIFIED or NOT_QUALIFIED)
   - D16: early_registration_assessments (Persists SCP screening exam and interview results)
   - D10: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail)

PROCESS: P7.0 Manage Sectioning & Enrollment
-> Inbound from Registrar: [Beginning of School Year Sectioning Plan] (Noun: Structured assignment map of approved learners to specific sections and advisers)
<- Inbound from DB: [Pending Enrollment Data] (Reads D5 enrollment_applications to retrieve the QUALIFIED or READY_FOR_SECTIONING learner pool)
<- Inbound from DB: [Learner Data] (Reads D1 learners for demographic verification during section assignment)
<- Inbound from DB: [Section Capacities] (Reads D18 sections to enforce maximum slot limits and prevent over-enrollment)
<- Inbound from DB: [Enrollment Window Status] (Reads D9 school_years to verify BOSY enrollment window is active before processing assignments)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [Official Class Roster & SF1 Report] (Noun: Finalized section class lists and preliminary School Form 1 data)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D17: enrollment_records (Persists definitive learner-to-section linkage: the Enrollment Record)
   - D5: enrollment_applications (Updates application status to ENROLLED)
   - D18: sections (Updates real-time section capacity metrics)
   - D22: section_advisers (Persists historical section adviser ledger entry)
   - D10: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail)

PROCESS: P8.0 Finalize EOSY Promotional Status
-> Inbound from Registrar: [EOSY Finalization Data] (Noun: Packet containing general average, academic status (PROMOTED / RETAINED / CONDITIONALLY_PROMOTED), and end-of-year health measurements)
<- Inbound from DB: [Learner Profile] (Reads D1 learners for student identity validation)
<- Inbound from DB: [Active Class Roster] (Reads D17 enrollment_records and D18 sections for final grading context and section lock eligibility)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [SF1 Register & SF5 Promotion Master] (Noun: Finalized School Form 1 class register and School Form 5 promotion master list for DepEd compliance)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D17: enrollment_records (Persists final promotional data: general average and academic status)
   - D18: sections (Applies isEosyFinalized lock to prevent further enrollment changes)
   - D19: health_records (Persists end-of-year student health measurements: height, weight, BMI)
   - D10: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail)
