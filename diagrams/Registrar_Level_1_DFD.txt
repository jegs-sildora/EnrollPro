================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING (SENIOR ARCHITECT AUDIT)
================================================================================

PROCESS: P6.0 Verify & Screen Applications
-> Inbound from Registrar: [Doc Verifications & Assess. Scores] (Noun: Checklist flags and numerical score packet)
<- Inbound from DB: [Pending Application Data] (Reads D2 to identify targets for verification)
<- Inbound from DB: [Rubric Criteria] (Reads D14 to validate scores against program logic)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [Verified Roster & Compliance Alerts] (Noun: List of qualified students and missing document notifications)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D15: application_checklists (Persists compliance booleans)
   - D2: early_registration_applications (Updates eligibilityState)
   - D16: early_registration_assessments (Persists screening results)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail)

PROCESS: P7.0 Manage Sectioning & Enrollment
-> Inbound from Registrar: [BOSY Sectioning Plan] (Noun: Assignments of students to specific sections)
<- Inbound from DB: [Pending Enrollment Data] (Reads D5 to get approved enrollment pool)
<- Inbound from DB: [Learner Data] (Reads D1 for demographic verification during sectioning)
<- Inbound from DB: [Section Capacities] (Reads D18 to prevent over-enrollment)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [Official Class Rosters & SF1 Data] (Noun: Finalized section lists and preliminary SF1)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D17: enrollment_records (Persists definitive section linkage)
   - D5: enrollment_applications (Updates status to ENROLLED)
   - D18: sections (Updates real-time capacity metrics)
   - D22: section_advisers (Persists historical advisory ledger)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail)

PROCESS: P8.0 Finalize EOSY Promotional Status
-> Inbound from Registrar: [EOSY Finalization Data] (Noun: Packet with general average and promotion status)
<- Inbound from DB: [Learner Profile] (Reads D1 for student identity validation)
<- Inbound from DB: [Active Class Roster] (Reads D17 and D18 for final grading context)
<- Outbound to Registrar: [SF1 Register & SF5 Promotion Master] (Noun: Finalized School Forms for DepEd compliance)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D17: enrollment_records (Persists final promotional data)
   - D18: sections (Applies finality lock to prevent further changes)
   - D19: health_records (Persists basic BMI/health measurements)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail)
