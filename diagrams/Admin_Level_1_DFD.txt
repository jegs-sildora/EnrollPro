================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING
================================================================================

PROCESS: P1.0 Configure School Settings
-> Inbound from Admin: [School Identity & School Year Lifecycle Parameters] (Branding, Logo, School Year Labels, Portal Dates)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Active School Year Lifecycle Status] (Current system state: DRAFT/ACTIVE)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D8: school_settings (Persists singleton UI and school metadata)
   - D9: school_years (Persists academic calendar and portal control flags)
   - D10: audit_logs (Strict DPA trail for configuration changes)

PROCESS: P2.0 Manage Users & Access
-> Inbound from Admin: [Personnel Credentials & Role Assignments] (Emails, RBAC roles, Advisory tasks)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Active User/Faculty Roster] (Searchable directory of accounts and faculty)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D11: users (Persists system login accounts and status)
   - D12: teachers (Persists physical identity of section advisers)
   - D13: teacher_designations (Persists School Year-scoped role and section mappings)
   - D10: audit_logs (Strict trail for account creation and privilege updates)

PROCESS: P3.0 Configure Special Programs (SCP)
-> Inbound from Admin: [SCP Criteria, Assessment Steps & Rubrics] (STE/SPA tracks, Formula, Screening Timeline)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Active Admission Pipelines] (Validated program tracks ready for applicant screening)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D14: scp_program_configs (Persists program-wide cutoff and formula metadata)
   - D15: scp_program_steps (Persists the sequential screening steps: Exam, Interview, etc.)
   - D16: scp_interview_rubric_categories (Persists the structured scoring metrics)
   - D10: audit_logs (Strict trail for curriculum-track eligibility changes)

PROCESS: P9.0 Finalize School Year
-> Inbound from Admin: [School Year Closing Command & Export Format] (Global lock trigger and LIS template selection)
-> Inbound from DB: [D17: Promotional Records] (Reads EOSY results: General Average, Status, Remarks)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Master DepEd LIS CSV & Completion Report] (LIS-ready CSV and School Year closure summary)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D9: school_years (Updates School Year status to ARCHIVED and sets isEosyFinalized lock)
   - D10: audit_logs (Strict trail for the final system-wide archival event)
