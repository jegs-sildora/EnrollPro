================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING (SENIOR ARCHITECT AUDIT)
================================================================================

PROCESS: P1.0 Configure School Settings
-> Inbound from Admin: [School Identity & School Year Lifecycle Parameters] (Noun: Metadata packet containing school name, logo, accent color, and academic year window dates)
<- Inbound from DB: [Active Configuration] (Reads D3 school_settings and D4 school_years to populate current state)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Active School Year Lifecycle Status Summary] (Noun: Report confirming current branding identity and enrollment window state)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D3: school_settings (Persists singleton school identity: name, logo, accent color)
   - D4: school_years (Persists academic calendar windows and enrollment phase dates)
   - D20: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: immutable security trail)

PROCESS: P2.0 Manage Users & Access
-> Inbound from Admin: [Personnel Credentials & Role Assignments] (Noun: Security packet with email, employee ID, role, and department assignment)
<- Inbound from DB: [Existing Personnel Data] (Reads from D1 users, D2 teachers, and D19 departments to enforce uniqueness)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Personnel Directory] (Noun: Searchable collection of system user accounts and linked teacher profiles)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D1: users (Persists system account credentials and RBAC role assignments)
   - D2: teachers (Persists teacher profile demographics and contact data)
   - D27: teacher_subjects (Persists teacher subject specializations)
   - D28: teacher_designations (Persists SY-scoped advisory and ancillary role assignments)
   - D20: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail for RBAC changes)

PROCESS: P3.0 Configure Special Programs (SCP)
-> Inbound from Admin: [SCP Criteria & Rubrics] (Noun: Configuration packet for STE/SPA admission logic, assessment steps, and scoring rubric criteria)
<- Inbound from DB: [Current Program Logic] (Reads D22 scp_program_configs and D23 scp_program_steps to retrieve existing state)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Configured Admission Pipelines] (Noun: Confirmation summary of active SCP admission tracks, steps, and rubric weights)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D22: scp_program_configs (Persists core SCP formula and cutoff logic)
   - D23: scp_program_steps (Persists ordered assessment pipeline steps)
   - D24: scp_interview_rubric_categories (Persists dynamic rubric categories)
   - D25: scp_interview_rubric_criteria (Persists individual scoring criteria)
   - D26: scp_program_options (Persists available program variants like Art Field or Sports List)
   - D20: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail for eligibility logic changes)

PROCESS: P9.0 Finalize School Year
-> Inbound from Admin: [School Year Closing Parameters] (Noun: Trigger parameters for global school year archival and LIS export generation)
<- Inbound from DB: [Promotional Records] (Reads D18 enrollment_records for final promotion status and general averages)
<- Inbound from DB: [School Year Context Data] (Reads D9 learners, D2 teachers, and D6 sections for DepEd LIS compliance)
<- Outbound to Admin: [LIS Export Package & Completion Report] (Noun: Final DepEd LIS CSV export package and statutory school year completion report)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D4: school_years (Updates school year status to ARCHIVED; isEosyFinalized = true)
   - D20: audit_logs (Audit Log Entry: security trail for year-end archival)
