================================================================================
DATA FLOW DICTIONARY & TABLE MAPPING (SENIOR ARCHITECT AUDIT)
================================================================================

PROCESS: P1.0 Configure School Settings
-> Inbound from Admin: [School Identity & School Year Lifecycle Parameters] (Noun: Metadata packet containing school name, logo, and active year dates)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Active School Year Lifecycle Status Summary] (Noun: Report confirming current system state)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D8: school_settings (Persists singleton UI and school identity)
   - D9: school_years (Persists academic calendar and enrollment windows)
   - D10: audit_logs (Immutable security trail)

PROCESS: P2.0 Manage Users & Access
-> Inbound from Admin: [Personnel Credentials & Role Assignments] (Noun: Security packet with email, role, and department)
<- Inbound from DB: [Existing Personnel Data] (Reads from D11 Users, D12 Teachers, and D21 Departments to prevent duplicates)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Personnel Directory] (Noun: Searchable collection of system accounts)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D11: users (Persists system accounts)
   - D12: teachers (Persists faculty demographics)
   - D13: teacher_designations (Persists advisory and ancillary role mappings)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail for RBAC changes)

PROCESS: P3.0 Configure Special Programs (SCP)
-> Inbound from Admin: [SCP Criteria & Rubrics] (Noun: Config packet for STE/SPA admission logic)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Configured Admission Pipelines] (Noun: Validation summary of active tracks)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D14: scp_program_configs (Persists formula, steps, and rubric criteria models)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail for eligibility logic changes)

PROCESS: P9.0 Finalize School Year
-> Inbound from Admin: [SY Closing Parameters] (Noun: Trigger parameters for global archival)
<- Inbound from DB: [Promotional Records] (Reads D17 for final grades and promotion status)
<- Inbound from DB: [System Context Data] (Reads D1 Learners, D12 Teachers, and D18 Sections for LIS compliance)
<- Outbound to Admin: [Master DepEd LIS CSV & Completion Report] (Noun: Final archival package and statutory reports)
-> Outbound to DB:
   - D9: school_years (Updates status to ARCHIVED)
   - D10: audit_logs (Security trail for year-end archival)
